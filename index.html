# Privacy Policy for Biblia Takatifu **Effective Date:** June 12, 2026 Biblia Takatifu is published by **AlHEl Apps**. Biblia Takatifu is an offline Bible reading application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. The app does not sell personal information and does not use personal information for advertising purposes. ## Information We Collect Biblia Takatifu may store user-created Bible study data, including: * Notes * Bookmarks * Highlights * Favorites * Recent references * App settings This information is stored solely to provide the app’s functionality and improve the user experience. ## Google Drive Backup If you choose to use the Google Drive backup and restore feature, Biblia Takatifu may transmit and store backup data in your Google Drive app data folder. Backup data may include: * Notes * Bookmarks * Highlights * Favorites * Related app settings and data This data is used only to back up and restore your Biblia Takatifu data. Biblia Takatifu does not access, read, modify, or manage your personal Google Drive files. Access is limited to the app-specific storage area required for backup and restore functionality. ## Local Storage Biblia Takatifu stores Bible reading data locally on your device. This may include: * Notes * Bookmarks * Highlights * Favorites * Recent references * App preferences and settings This information remains on your device and can be removed by deleting the data within the app or uninstalling the application. ## Data Sharing Biblia Takatifu does not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information with third parties for advertising, marketing, or commercial purposes. ## Data Control Users can manage their data directly within the app. You may: * Edit or delete notes * Add or remove bookmarks * Add or remove highlights * Manage favorites * Clear recent references If Google Drive backup is enabled, you may also remove backup data from your Google Account or Google Drive. ## Children's Privacy Biblia Takatifu is intended for general audiences. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken. ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
